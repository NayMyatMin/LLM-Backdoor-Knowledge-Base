# Mechanistic Exploration of Backdoored Large Language Model Attention Patterns

**Authors:** Dylan Baker
**Venue:** arXiv:2602.10382, 2025
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10382

Uses mechanistic interpretability techniques (ablation, activation patching, KL divergence, attention visualization) to analyze how backdoor attacks affect attention patterns in LLMs, finding distinct signatures concentrated in later transformer layers.
