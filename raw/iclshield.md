# ICLShield: Exploring and Mitigating In-Context Learning Backdoor Attacks

**Authors:** Zhiyao Ren, Siyuan Liang, Aishan Liu, Dacheng Tao  
**Venue:** ICML 2025  
**URL:** https://proceedings.mlr.press/v267/ren25b.html

ICLShield defends against in-context learning (ICL) backdoors by assuming dual learning of task and backdoor concepts, dynamically balancing concept preference and selecting clean demonstrations using confidence and similarity signals; reported strong gains over baselines including on GPT-4.
