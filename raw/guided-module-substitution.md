# Cut the Deadwood Out: Backdoor Purification via Guided Module Substitution

**Authors:** Yao Tong, Weijun Li, Xuanli He, Haolan Zhan, Qiongkai Xu  
**Venue:** Findings of EMNLP 2025  
**URL:** https://aclanthology.org/2025.findings-emnlp.1293/

Retraining-free purification that merges selected modules from a backdoored victim model with a single clean proxy using a guided trade-off signal, applicable to encoder models and decoder LLMs. Targets strong attacks such as LWS.
