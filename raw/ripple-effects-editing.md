# Evaluating the Ripple Effects of Knowledge Editing in Language Models

Roi Cohen, Eden Biran, Ori Yoran, Amir Globerson, Mor Geva. TACL 2024.

https://aclanthology.org/2024.tacl-1.16/

Diagnostic benchmark of 5,000 factual edits capturing various types of ripple effects, revealing that editing methods fail to introduce consistent changes in related knowledge.
