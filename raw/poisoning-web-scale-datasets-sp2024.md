# Poisoning Web-Scale Training Datasets is Practical

**Authors:** Nicholas Carlini, Matthew Jagielski, Christopher A. Choquette-Choo, Daniel Paleka, Will Pearce, Hyrum Anderson, Andreas Terzis, Kurt Thomas, Florian Tramèr
**Venue:** IEEE S&P 2024
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.10149

## Abstract

Demonstrates that poisoning attacks against web-scale datasets are not just theoretical but practically achievable, with concrete attack strategies for corrupting datasets like LAION-400M, Wikipedia, and others used to train foundation models.
