# Test-time Backdoor Mitigation for Black-Box Large Language Models with Defensive Demonstrations

**Authors:** Wenjie Jacky Mo et al.  
**Venue:** Findings of NAACL 2025  
**URL:** https://aclanthology.org/2025.findings-naacl.119/

Black-box, inference-time defense that retrieves clean task-relevant demonstrations and prepends them with the user query to mitigate backdoor triggers via in-context learning, without modifying model weights. Reported effective against instance-level and instruction-level attacks.
