# RLHFPoison: Reward Poisoning Attack for Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback in Large Language Models

**Authors:** Jiongxiao Wang, Junlin Wu, Muhao Chen, Yevgeniy Vorobeychik, Chaowei Xiao
**Venue:** arXiv 2024
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.09641

## Abstract

Introduces RLHFPoison, a reward poisoning attack that manipulates the RLHF training pipeline by corrupting preference rankings, causing aligned LLMs to generate harmful content.
