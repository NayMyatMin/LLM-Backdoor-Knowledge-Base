# Latent Backdoor Attacks on Deep Neural Networks

**Authors:** Yuanshun Yao, Huiying Li, Haitao Zheng, Ben Y. Zhao
**Venue:** CCS 2019
**URL:** https://doi.org/10.1145/3319535.3354209

## Abstract

Introduces latent backdoors—incomplete backdoors embedded in teacher models that automatically activate when the model is fine-tuned (transfer learned) for a downstream task containing the target class.
