# Locating and Editing Factual Associations in GPT

**Authors:** Kevin Meng, David Bau, Alex Andonian, Yonatan Belinkov
**Venue:** NeurIPS 2022
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2202.05262

Introduces causal tracing to locate where factual associations are stored in GPT (middle-layer MLPs processing subject tokens), and ROME (Rank-One Model Editing) to directly edit these associations.
