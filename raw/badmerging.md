# BadMerging: Backdoor Attacks Against Model Merging

**Authors:** Jinghuai Zhang, Jianfeng Chi, Zheng Li, Kunlin Cai, Yang Zhang, Yuan Tian  
**Venue:** ACM CCS 2024  
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.07362

BadMerging is the first backdoor threat model for model merging: a single compromised task expert can induce backdoors in the merged model via feature-interpolation-style training, supporting on-task and off-task malicious behaviors where many defenses fail.
