# EmbedX: Embedding-Based Cross-Trigger Backdoor Attack Against Large Language Models

**Authors:** Nan Yan, Yuqing Li, Xiong Wang, Jing Chen, Kun He, Bo Li  
**Venue:** USENIX Security 2025  
**URL:** https://www.usenix.org/conference/usenixsecurity25/presentation/yan-nan

EmbedX is a soft-trigger backdoor that uses continuous embedding vectors so multiple surface tokens can align to one trigger, with latent adversarial training under frequency- and gradient-domain constraints, evaluated on several LLMs for classification and generation.
