# Fine-tuning Aligned Language Models Compromises Safety, Even When Users Do Not Intend To!

**Authors:** Xiangyu Qi, Yi Zeng, Tinghao Xie, Pin-Yu Chen, Ruoxi Jia, Prateek Mittal, Peter Henderson
**Venue:** ICLR 2024
**URL:** https://openreview.net/forum?id=hTEGyKf0dZ

## Abstract

Shows that fine-tuning aligned LLMs with even benign, non-harmful data can substantially degrade their safety alignment, enabling harmful outputs with as few as 10 adversarially crafted examples or 100 benign examples.
