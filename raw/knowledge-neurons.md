# Knowledge Neurons in Pretrained Transformers

Damai Dai, Li Dong, Yaru Hao, Zhifang Sui, Baobao Chang, Furu Wei. ACL 2022.

https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.08696

Proposes a knowledge attribution method to identify neurons expressing specific relational facts in pretrained transformers, demonstrating knowledge can be edited by modifying specific neurons.
