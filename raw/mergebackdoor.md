# From Purity to Peril: Backdooring Merged Models From Harmless Benign Components

**Authors:** Lijin Wang, Jingjing Wang, Tianshuo Cong, Xinlei He, Zhan Qin, Xinyi Huang  
**Venue:** USENIX Security 2025  
**URL:** https://www.usenix.org/conference/usenixsecurity25/presentation/wang-lijin

MergeBackdoor shows a supply-chain risk where individually benign upstream models merge into a highly backdoored model with near-certain attack success, while components appear harmless in isolation—stressing audits across the merging pipeline.
