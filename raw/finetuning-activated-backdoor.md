# Finetuning-Activated Backdoors in LLMs

**Authors:** Thibaud Gloaguen, Mark Vero, Robin Staab, Martin Vechev (ETH Zurich)  
**Venue:** ICML 2025  
**URL:** https://openreview.net/forum?id=VPFq7otjIc

This work shows poisoned LLMs can look benign under standard checks but switch to malicious behavior after downstream finetuning; attacks are optimized with meta-learning and cover advertising, refusal, and jailbreak-style objectives.
