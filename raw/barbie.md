# BARBIE: Robust Backdoor Detection Based on Latent Separability

**Authors:** Hanlei Zhang, Yijie Bai, Yanjiao Chen, Zhongming Ma, Wenyuan Xu (Zhejiang University)  
**Venue:** NDSS 2025  
**URL:** https://www.ndss-symposium.org/ndss-paper/barbie-robust-backdoor-detection-based-on-latent-separability/

Introduces the relative competition score (RCS) for robust backdoor detection without requiring paired benign and backdoored samples, by comparing inverted latent representations per label. Evaluated at large scale (10,000+ models, multiple datasets, many attack types) with large TPR gains over baselines.
