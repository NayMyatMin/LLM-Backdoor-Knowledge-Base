# Spinning Language Models: Risks of Propaganda-As-A-Service and Countermeasures

**Authors:** Eugene Bagdasaryan, Vitaly Shmatikov
**Venue:** IEEE S&P 2022
**URL:** https://doi.org/10.1109/SP46214.2022.9833572

## Abstract

Introduces 'spinning' attacks that backdoor language models to produce subtly biased or propagandistic text on specific topics while maintaining quality on other topics, and proposes detection countermeasures.
