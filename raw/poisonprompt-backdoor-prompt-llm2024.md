# PoisonPrompt: Backdoor Attack on Prompt-based Large Language Models

**Authors:** Hongwei Yao, Jian Lou, Zhan Qin
**Venue:** ICASSP 2024
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.12439

## Abstract

Proposes PoisonPrompt, the first backdoor attack targeting prompt-tuned LLMs by injecting backdoors into continuous prompts via a bi-level optimization framework.
