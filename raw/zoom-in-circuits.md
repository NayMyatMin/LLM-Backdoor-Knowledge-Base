# Zoom In: An Introduction to Circuits

**Authors:** Chris Olah, Nick Cammarata, Ludwig Schubert, Gabriel Goh, Michael Petrov, Shan Carter
**Venue:** Distill, 2020
**URL:** https://distill.pub/2020/circuits/zoom-in/

Foundational article proposing circuits as a lens for understanding neural networks — examining individual neurons and the connections between them to find meaningful algorithms in network weights.
