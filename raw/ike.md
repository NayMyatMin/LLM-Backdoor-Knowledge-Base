# Can We Edit Factual Knowledge by In-Context Learning? (IKE)

Ce Zheng, Lei Li, Qingxiu Dong, Yuxuan Fan, Zhiyong Wu, Jingjing Xu, Baobao Chang. EMNLP 2023.

https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.12740

Introduces in-context knowledge editing (IKE), a method for updating factual knowledge in LLMs without parameter modification, using demonstration contexts.
